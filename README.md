# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

An interactive Human Resources analytics dashboard built in Tableau to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and key HR metrics.

The project transforms raw HR data into actionable insights that help organizations monitor workforce trends, improve employee retention, and support strategic HR decision-making.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee data to understand workforce composition, identify drivers of employee attrition, and evaluate workforce satisfaction across departments, education fields, age groups, and job roles.

The dashboard provides HR managers with a centralized view of key workforce metrics and supports data-driven talent management strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

- **Monitor employee attrition**
- **Understand workforce demographics**
- **Identify high-risk departments**
- **Analyze job satisfaction**
- **Compare attrition across age groups and gender**
- **Support employee retention strategies**

---

## Dashboard Features

### Workforce KPIs

- Employee Count
- Active Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Employee Age

### Workforce Analysis

- Department-wise Attrition
- Employee Distribution by Age
- Education Field Analysis
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Age Group
- Job Satisfaction Heatmap

## Key Insights

- Sales department experienced the highest employee attrition.
- Employees aged 25–34 represented the largest workforce segment.
- Life Sciences employees accounted for the highest attrition by education field.
- Job satisfaction varied considerably across job roles.
- The dashboard enables HR teams to identify trends and make informed retention decisions.
